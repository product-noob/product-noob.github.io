If you landed here without knowing me at all, here is a little something about me.

I graduated in Electrical Engineering from **MNIT Jaipur**, but my fascination with technology led me straight into software development at **Oracle** .There, I learned the craft of building scalable systems and helped create the first version of the [Data Governance Studio](https://docs.oracle.com/cd/E93133_01/PDF/8.0.6.0.0/OFSAA_DM_Pack_DGS_US_Regulatory_Reporting_8.0.6.0.0_User_Guide.pdf) — an experience that gave me a solid foundation in how technology actually comes to life.

Somewhere along the way, I realized I was as curious about the *why* behind products as I was about the *how*. So, fueled by curiosity (and a hint of well-placed FOMO), I took a leap into business school — pursuing an MBA at **FMS Delhi**. During my time there, I explored multiple paths: from interning with **Citibank’s [Treasury & Trade Solutions](https://www.citibank.com/tts)** team to competing in national case competitions, including **winning ₹5 lakhs and presenting on national television** at the *[Reckitt Benckiser Case Challenge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=180lua2YSTI&t=502s)*. It was an exciting detour that reaffirmed one thing — my heart belonged closer to technology and product.

Post-MBA, I joined **Paytm** as a Product Manager, diving into several 0-to-1 journeys. I helped build the *["Paytm Mini Apps Store"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nuK7Ct59Vyk&t=3161s)*, turned the *[Vaccine Slot Finder](https://princejain.me/2021/08/03/Building-Vaccine-Slot-Finder.html)*'s crazy idea into a nationwide utility during the pandemic, and laid the foundation for the *Paytm Health* vertical. These experiences shaped my love for solving ambiguous problems that create tangible user and business impact.

After nearly three years at Paytm, I moved to **Flipkart**, India’s leading e-commerce platform, to explore product building at scale. Over the past four years, I’ve worked across two distinctly exciting charters:  
- **Growth Initiatives:** Driving rapid, low-cost experiments across the funnel to boost acquisition, retention, and GMV.  
- **GenAI :** I now lead all major conversational Gen-AI product initatives across Flipkart, currently working on building an Agentic GenAI conversational shopping experience which has evolved from our earlier learnings of building *[Flippi](https://inc42.com/buzz/flipkart-launches-chatgpt-powered-shopping-assistant-flippi/)* — Flipkart’s conversational AI shopping assistant — where we’re blending e-commerce and generative AI to redefine how millions of Indians discover and shop online.

As a product manager, I thrive on connecting the dots between user intent, business goals, and technical possibilities — crafting products that not only solve real problems but also shape how users experience technology. I thrive at the intersection of data, design, and strategy — and lately, I’ve been especially drawn to the potential of **Generative AI** in transforming how users interact with technology.

When I’m not building or breaking things at work, you’ll usually find me:
- Reading (and very occasionaly writing) about tech, AI, and product strategy  
- Experimenting with GenAI tools (or occasionally building my own — like a [ChatGPT-powered Meet Summarizer](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/chatgpt-google-meet-summa/kofkiemddfpekcadmaeheonbbkhnclhj), a [ChatLLM replica](https://mycustomchatgpt.streamlit.app/), or a few “borderline useful” office automations [tools]((https://princejain.me/tools/)  
- Scrolling through X (Twitter)  
- Or unwinding by cooking something new — my favorite escape from the everyday hustle.
